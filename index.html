1.Introduction

Our project is intended to detect the location of fault in underground cable lines from the base station kilometers away from the place where fault occured.

We display our fault result even send the data into the concerned station.













Let's talk little about Faults in Underground cable
2.Background of our project

In the urban areas, the electrical cable runs underground instead of overhead lines. Whenever the fault occurs in underground cable it is difficult to detect the exact location of the fault for process of repairing that particular cable. The proposed system detects the exact location of the fault and by the means of IOT

When we talk about the term IOT it is the best way of mitigating any problem as through this all object become interconnected and smart.

3. Objective

The main objective of our project is to detect the underground cable's fault and sent the data to the stations So that underground fault can be found exactly from the base station in km internet which makes the repairing process easier and less time to fix the problem.

4. Scope and Limitations

Since Urban cities,Factories,Plants,Airports are increasing day by day most of them choose Underground cable system.

More the use of Underground cable more chances of being fault in cable and more use of our system.

Every system is shifting towards IOT.

Limitation is though the detection can be done to solve the fault we need human touch to solve it.

Expected outcome

The faults distance detection system shows the different outcomes of fault in LCD display of the system and followed by mobile webpage through IOT Wi-Fi module as per case chosen.

This system consists of fault sensing circuit having set of 4 series resistors in each phase of the line equally divided into 4 parts at a distance of 1 km each where a slider switch is connected to the supply. when the switch is closed the current flows through the resistors and voltage drop occurs which indicates that Less maintenance.
